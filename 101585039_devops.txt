COMP2156 course is about learning different protocols and procedures that
DevOps engineer use to help and improve the productivity in the workplace.
It has 8 lecture quizzes, 8 lab exercises, two assignments (one by groups
and another indivudual), and two exams (midterm and final).
The lab exercieses help understand more and practice what we learned in the 
lecture, having different steps to follow to complete the tasks successfully.
