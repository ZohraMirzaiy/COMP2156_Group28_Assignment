COMP2156 course is about learning different protocols and procedures that
DevOps engineer use to help and improve the productivity in the workplace.
It includes 8 lecture quizzes, 8 lab exercises, two assignments (one groups
and one indivudual), and two exams (midterm and final).
The lab exercieses help students practice and understand more what was 
taught in the lectures, providing different step-by-step instructions
to complete the tasks successfully.
