This file contain information about the George Brown College.

George Brown College is a well-known public college located in the heart of downtown Toronto.

It is recognized for its strong focus on hands-on, career-oriented education.
The college has close connections with industry partners, helping students gain real-world experience.

George Brown offers diverse programs in technology, business, health, and creative fields.
Its downtown location also provides excellent access to jobs, networking, and city life.
For international students, however, the tuition cost compared to what is actually delivered can feel extremely high.
In many cases, the value received does not seem proportional to the amount paid.

Despite the college’s strong reputation, the quality of teaching can vary significantly.
In some courses, professors do not properly explain the content, relying heavily on pre-made PDFs instead of delivering structured and engaging lectures that support real learning.

Students are often required to attend classes in person, even when the learning experience could be delivered remotely.
Many spend up to one and a half hours commuting, only to arrive and realize that the class consists mainly of reading a PDF independently, with little guidance or interaction.

This combination of high tuition costs, long commutes, and limited instructional value creates frustration, especially for international students.
Over time, it reinforces the perception that the effort, time, and money invested do not always translate into a learning experience that meets expectations.
