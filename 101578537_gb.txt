This file contain information about the George Brown College.

George Brown College is a well-known public college located in the heart of downtown Toronto.

It is recognized for its strong focus on hands-on, career-oriented education.
The college has close connections with industry partners, helping students gain real-world experience.

George Brown offers diverse programs in technology, business, health, and creative fields.
Its downtown location also provides excellent access to jobs, networking, and city life.
For international students, however, the tuition cost compared to what is actually delivered can feel extremely high.
In many cases, the value received does not seem proportional to the amount paid.
