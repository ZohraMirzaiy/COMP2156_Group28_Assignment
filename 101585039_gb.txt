George Brown College has three locations around Toronto's downtown: St. James,
Waterfront and Casa Loma. It has a variety of more than 200 programs across the 
campuses and currently has more than 30,000 full time students.
Its School of Computer Technology is located at 160 Kendal Avenue, near Dupont 
subway station, making it easy for students to commute to college.
The recentley named George Brown Polythecnic provides computer labs, modern
facilities and a supportive learning environment, helping students gain
the necessary skills for each career.
