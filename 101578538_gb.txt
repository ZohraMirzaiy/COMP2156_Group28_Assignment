George Brown College is a public college in downtown Toronto. It’s known for being practical and career-focused, so a lot of the learning is hands-on instead of just theory.

George Brown has multiple campuses across Toronto, mostly around the downtown area, which makes it feel very connected to the city. The programs are pretty diverse, from business and health to technology and skilled trades.

Many students there are focused on building a career fast, so you’ll see a mix of recent high school grads and people coming back to study to switch careers or upgrade their skills.

One thing that stands out is how practical the learning approach is. A lot of courses include labs, group work, and applied assignments that simulate real-world scenarios, which helps students feel more prepared for internships and entry-level roles.

Because the college is located in Toronto, there are also more chances to connect with companies, attend events, and look for co-op or part-time opportunities related to your program.
