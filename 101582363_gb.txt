George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Canada. It offers a wide range of programs in technology, business, health sciences, hospitality, and design. The college focuses on hands-on, career-oriented education that helps students develop practical skills for the job market. Many programs are designed in partnership with industry, so the curriculum matches real-world expectations. As an international student, I appreciate the support services, modern labs, and downtown campuses that connect education with the city and employers.
George Brown has multiple campuses across Toronto.
